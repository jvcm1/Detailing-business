About Us

Detail Delivery specializes in pressure washing water lines, wax coatings, and general cleaning for boats. Our mobile service ensures your boat gets the care it deserves without you having to lift a finger.

Our Packages

  • Basic Clean: Includes general cleaning and pressure washing of the exterior.
  • Wax Coating: General cleaning, pressure washing, and a high-quality wax coating.
  • Full Detail: Comprehensive cleaning, pressure washing, wax coating, and interior detailing.

Subscription Options

Keep your boat in pristine condition year-round with our subscription services:

  • Monthly: Regular monthly cleaning and maintenance.
  • Quarterly: Cleaning and maintenance every three months.
  • Annual: Comprehensive cleaning and maintenance once a year.
